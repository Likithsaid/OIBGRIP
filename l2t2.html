Tribute to Stan Lee

Who is Stan Lee?

Stan Lee was an American comic book writer, editor, publisher, and producer. He is best known for co-creating many of the Marvel Comics characters we know and love today, including Spider-Man, Iron Man, the Hulk, Black Widow, and the X-Men. Lee passed away on November 12, 2018 at the age of 95.

Legacy

Stan Lee's contributions to the world of comics and pop culture cannot be overstated. He helped revolutionize the comic book industry and created characters that have become household names. His work has inspired countless writers, artists, and filmmakers, and his legacy will continue to live on for generations to come.
